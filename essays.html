Media, Information, and Technology Literacy.

The difference between the three
  • Media Literacy is one's ability to decode media messages, understanding the media that you create and consume
  • Information Literacy is one's ability to recognize the "what, when, and where" when locating the needed information. See Stages for more details.
  • Technology Literacy is one's ability to utilize all current techonologies in communicating and acquiring information
